University Adoption
HIGH

↳ Start with dept heads not central IT. Free pilot removes barrier. Faculty champions bypass bureaucracy.
🔒
Data Privacy Concerns
HIGH
Universities may refuse on principle. One breach destroys trust entirely.
↳ Third-party security audit pre-launch. FERPA certification. IRB advisory board from Day 1.
🌐
Cold Start Problem
HIGH
With 1–2 universities the product has no value. Why would anyone search?
↳ Pre-load with public thesis data. Partner 3 universities simultaneously. Boston cluster strategy.
⚔️
Incumbent Competition
MEDIUM

Quantum Dependency
