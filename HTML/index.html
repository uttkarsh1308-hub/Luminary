Privacy & Compliance

"Your data never moves. The intelligence does."

🔒

FERPA Compliant by Design

No raw student or research data ever leaves the university's servers. Compliance is architectural — violation is structurally impossible, not just policy.

🏥

IRB Friendly

No cross-institutional data transfer means no IRB amendment required for discovery. Each university retains full control of its research records.

⚛️

Quantum Encryption

Walsh-Hadamard Transform + Phase Encoding + CNOT entanglement. Lossless, reversible encoding that makes raw data unreadable without the university's private key.
