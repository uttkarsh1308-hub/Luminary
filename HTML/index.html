FERPA Compliant by Design

No raw student or research data leaves university servers. Compliance is architectural — violation is structurally impossible, not just policy.

Risk Analysis

5 Risks — Identified and Mitigated

🏛
University Adoption
HIGH
Slow procurement cycles. IT approval takes 6–18 months. Research VPs change frequently.
↳ Start with dept heads not central IT. Free pilot removes barrier. Faculty champions bypass bureaucracy.
🔒
Data Privacy Concerns
HIGH
Universities may refuse on principle. One breach destroys trust entirely.
↳ Third-party security audit pre-launch. FERPA certification. IRB advisory board from Day 1.
🌐
Cold Start Problem
HIGH
With 1–2 universities the product has no value — why would anyone search?
↳ Pre-load with public thesis data. Partner 3 universities simultaneously. Boston cluster strategy.
⚔️
Incumbent Competition
MEDIUM
Elsevier, Clarivate or Microsoft could build this with more resources.
↳ Federated architecture is 2-year head start. University relationships are the moat. First mover advantage.
⚛️
Quantum Dependency
MEDIUM
QAOA classically simulated in prototype. Real quantum hardware is expensive and unstable.
↳ Classical simulation performs identically at demo scale. Architecture is quantum-ready not quantum-dependent.
Handling Uncertainty in Conversations
